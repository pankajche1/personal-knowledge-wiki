How Lifetraps Change

Steps:

  1. Label and Identify your Lifetraps
  • Subjugation
  • Defectiveness
  • Failure
  • Social Exclusion

  1. Understand the Childhood Origins of Your Lifetrap. Feel the Wounded Child Inside You.

  2. The Third Step is to Build a Case Against Your Lifetrap. Disprove its Validity at a Rational Level.

  3. Write Letters to the Parent, Sibling, or Peer Who Helped Cause Your Lifetrap.

  4. Examine Your Lifetrap Pattern in Careful Detail.

  5. The Next Step is Pattern-breaking.
